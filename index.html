Questions

Frequently asked

Is this safe to use in public?

Technically yes — the audio is suggestive but not explicit. Practically? Use headphones unless you want to be that person on the train.

Do I need an account?

Nope. Anonymous play works fine. Sign in only if you want your scores, badges, and purchased voices to sync across devices.

How do purchases work?

One-time purchases — buy a voice pack once, own it forever. No subscriptions, no recurring fees.

Is the Custom Voice Studio creepy?

Your recordings stay on your device — they never touch our servers. We literally cannot listen to them. We don't want to.

What's coming next?

An Android app on Google Play with native haptics + Play Billing. iOS later. More voice packs over time. Send requests to support@shakegasm.com.

Can I delete my account?

Yes — one tap inside the app. Account is recoverable for 30 days, then permanently erased.
